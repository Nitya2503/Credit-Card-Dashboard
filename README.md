# Credit-Card-Dashboard

Short Description

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze weekly credit card transactions and customer behavior. The solution delivers real-time insights into revenue, transaction volume, interest earned, customer demographics, and card usage patterns to support data-driven decision-making for financial institutions.

Features

Business Problem:

There is no single place to view and analyze credit card transactions and customer behavior. This makes it hard to understand spending trends, identify important customer groups, spot performance issues, or see which card categories are doing well. Without these insights, it becomes difficult to make informed decisions for improving marketing, managing risks, and growing the credit card portfolio.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals:

- Key KPIs Revenue - 55.3 M Total Transaction - 45 M Total Interest - 7.8 M Income - 576 M Customer Satisfaction - 3.19.
- Interactive slicers lets users filter all visuals by selected Gender, Card Type, Income Range, Transaction Type, Quarter and date.
- A detailed table showing Card Category, Revenue, Total transaction and interest earned for that card category. 
- Revenue By Expenditure Type , Education, Customer Job, Card Category, Use Chip (column Chart) Displays the distribution of Revenue by Expenditure Type( ex Bills, Entertainment etc) , Education level(Graduate, High School etc),Customer Job (Businessman, SelfEmployee etc), Card Category(Silver, Platinum etc), Use Chip(Swipe, Online,Swipe).
- QTR Revenue and sum of total transaction count (Combo Charts) Displays sum of revenue and total transactions for each quarter.
- Revenue By Week (Line Chart) Displays weekly revenue split by male and female customers.
- A tabular view containing customer job type, salary details, interest earned, and the total salary amount.
- Revenue by Age Group, Income, Dependent Count, Top 5 States, Marital Status, Education level (Stacked Column Chart) Stacked Column chart showing: Revenue insights categorized by multiple attributes and split by gender.
  
Business Impact :

- Targeted Campaigns: Marketers can identify high-value customer segments (e.g., high-income groups, 40‚Äì60 age range, businessmen) and design personalized offers for maximum engagement.
- Product Positioning: Card teams can promote the most profitable card categories (e.g., Blue, Silver) to specific demographics based on spending and transaction behavior.
- Channel Strategy: Insights into swipe, chip, and online usage help optimize marketing budgets by focusing on the most-used transaction channels.
- Segment-Based Promotions: Spending patterns across Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, and Grocery enable highly focused promotional campaigns that increase revenue uplift.
- Geographic Targeting: Regional revenue insights help direct campaigns toward high-performing states and identify areas where additional marketing efforts can drive growth.
